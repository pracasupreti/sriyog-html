Tracking and Cookies Data

Using cookies and similar tracking technologies, we track the activities of our services and hold certain information.

Cookies are files with small amount of data which may include anonymous unique identifiers and are sent to your browsers from a website and stored on your devices. Tracking technologies use tags and scripts to collect information to track helping to improve our services.

Instructing your browser to refuse all cookies or monitoring the cookies can be done. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our services that may depend on your available cookies.

Examples of cookies we use:

  • Session Cookies – Session cookies are used to operate our services.
  • Preference Cookies – Preference cookies are used to remember your preferences and various settings.
  • Security Cookies –  Security cookies are used for security purposes.
